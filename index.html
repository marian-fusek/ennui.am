MIUNĀE

Eastern European beautyMade in RomaniaBuilt around time
The project

A complete skincare brand built from heritage, standards and plants given time — expressed through identity, language, packaging, imagery and digital.

Opening film / Brand atmosphere
01 / The brief

Build a beauty brand with nothing borrowed.

MIUNĀE began with Romanian heritage, a grandmother’s remedies and a refusal to accept diluted skincare. The task was to turn that truth into a brand that felt contemporary without sanding away its origin.

Nature has its own clock. We built the brand around time.

A voice with standards.

Linda’s copy approach kept the emotional truth of inherited remedies while refusing the soft-focus language that dominates skincare. It had to be sensorial, informed and certain.

01

The tension

Heritage without nostalgia. The brand carries memory and origin, but never performs folklore or leans on old-world romance as a shortcut.

02

The voice

Direct, intelligent and physically grounded. The language names texture, feeling and standards clearly — then gets out of the way.

03

What we refused

No vague luxury promises. No trend-bait wellness vocabulary. No poetry that floats away from the product and what it actually is.

04

How it scales

The same personality moves from founder story to packaging support, product education, social captions and launch films without becoming repetitive.

“You’re gonna wanna feel this.”

A small line that captures the whole balance: intimate without being soft, confident without shouting and sensorial without losing credibility.

One truth.
Many forms.

Every output came back to the same center: time, tactility, potency and the confidence of Eastern European beauty.

01

Identity

The hourglass lives inside the wordmark — not as decoration, but as the central idea made visible.

02

Packaging

Frosted glass, deep botanical color and disciplined typography make the products feel collected rather than manufactured.

03

Imagery

Clean architecture meets overgrown nature. Hard light, true color and living material push against polished surfaces.

04

Digital

The website behaves like the brand speaks: confident, layered and clear enough to let product education become part of the experience.

End of case study / MIUNĀE
Case study / 02 AIMS / Digital system

AIMS

Product storyDigital systemSales clarity
The project

A clearer digital story for a technically advanced product — designed to make depth feel immediate, useful and easy to trust.

Opening motion / Homepage lead-in
01 / The brief

Make technical depth feel immediate.

AIMS needed a clearer digital story and a more cohesive system — one capable of explaining a sophisticated product without making the experience feel dense, overworked or generic.

Product story,
one frame at a time.

The long pages are held inside one calm panoramic viewport. Drag directly, use the arrows or scroll with a trackpad.

Website / 01—07

Complex product.
Calm language.

Linda’s copy approach reduced friction rather than adding explanation. The writing needed to respect the intelligence of the tool while keeping every screen readable at a glance.

01

Lead with value

The clearest user benefit appears first. Technical depth follows only when it earns its place and helps the person make the next decision.

02

Calm, exact, modern

The tone avoids startup hype and dense feature dumping. Short sentences do the work, then leave the product room to demonstrate itself.

03

UI copy that disappears

Labels and microcopy are designed to guide behavior without becoming another visual layer users have to process.

04

One message system

Website, sales materials and social previews inherit the same hierarchy so the brand remains clear wherever the story begins.

Less explanation.
More orientation.

The best product writing lowers cognitive load, sharpens the proposition and lets sophisticated technology feel more sophisticated by sounding less performative.

The same logic,
outside the site.

Supporting assets inherit the same composure — clear hierarchy, generous space and a visual system that can move without losing recognition.

Social system / 01—03

One more
touchpoint.

The system stays recognizably AIMS when it leaves the screen.

End of case study / AIMS
