01 / MIUNĀE

MIUNĀE

The brief

Build a skincare brand from zero — identity, language, packaging direction, art direction and digital — without falling into beauty cliché. The answer was a world shaped by time, potency and Eastern European directness.

Opening frame

The world in
motion.

The first frame needed to carry the whole proposition: elegant, tactile, alive and slightly feral underneath the polish.

01 Motion / brand atmosphere
Selected visuals

Identity, web,
social and system.

Five frames that show how the same idea stretches across touchpoints without losing its center.

How it held together

One truth.
Many forms.

Every decision came back to time, tactility, standards and the confidence of a brand that does not need to oversell itself.

Identity

The hourglass lives inside the wordmark — not as decoration, but as the core idea made visible.

Packaging

Frosted glass, deep botanical color and exact typography make the products feel collected rather than manufactured.

Language

Direct, intelligent and emotionally specific. No generic skincare softness, no filler, no fog.

Words with
a spine.

Linda built a voice that carries Nadine’s energy without turning it into theater: blunt where beauty copy is usually vague, sensory without becoming ornamental and informed without sounding clinical. Marian built the visual world to hold the same tension — elegant, exact and alive. Together, the work became coherent because the standards were shared from the start.

Live

A brand that looks
how it feels.

From packaging to digital to launch materials, the world feels authored all the way through.
