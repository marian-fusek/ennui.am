And Then is provided as a personal movie and series journal. You may use it to search public catalog information and privately record your own viewing experiences.

Your notes, ratings, tags and other journal content belong to you. The app stores the archive locally and offers backup and export tools. You are responsible for maintaining a separate backup of information you do not want to lose.

Optional purchases

The core archive remains free. The app may offer consumable one-time tips and a non-consumable Founding Supporter purchase through Apple.

Consumable tips are contributions and do not create a recurring subscription or unlock the Founding Supporter badge. Founding Supporter adds a permanent in-app mark that can be restored with the Apple account used for purchase.

Billing, refunds and payment disputes are handled according to Apple’s terms and processes.
