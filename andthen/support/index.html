Before writing

A few things to try.

Catalog or artwork is not loading

Confirm that the iPhone is online, close and reopen the app and try the title again. Your already-saved local archive should remain available while the live catalog is unavailable.

Restore Founding Supporter

Open the Support screen in And Then and choose Restore Founding Supporter while signed into the same Apple account used for the purchase.

Protect the archive

Use Settings → Data Management to create a JSON backup before changing devices, removing the app or testing a major update.

A reminder did not appear

Check iOS Settings → Notifications → And Then. Notifications are requested only after you create a reminder.

A title is missing

Use the manual entry option to keep a private record from memory. The title can remain independent of the live catalog.

Purchase or refund question

Apple processes billing and refunds. Send us the product name and what the app displayed, but never send payment-card information.

Does And Then have an account or password?

No. The current app is local-first and does not require an And Then account.

Can support see my notes?

No. Personal notes are not automatically available to support. Only share an export if you intentionally choose to and it is necessary for a specific issue.

Can a deleted local archive be recovered?

Only from a backup or recovery snapshot that still exists. Create regular exports for anything you do not want to lose.
