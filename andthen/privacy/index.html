This policy describes the current And Then iOS app and this website. It explains what remains on your device and when an outside service is contacted.

Summary

And Then is designed as a local-first movie and series journal. It does not require an And Then account, does not contain advertising and does not use third-party analytics or cross-app tracking in the current release.

Your personal journal content is not intentionally sent to the And Then catalog service or TheTVDB.

Information stored on your device

The app may store the following locally:

  • Saved movies, series, plans, favorites and collections
  • Ratings, notes, favorite moments, moods, tags, people, locations and viewing context
  • Known or unknown watch dates, rewatch history and active watching sessions
  • Appearance, notification and product settings
  • Cached public movie, series and artwork information

This information remains until you edit it, delete it, erase the app or restore a different backup.

Catalog requests and artwork

When you search or open public movie or television information, the app sends the search words or TheTVDB title identifier to the And Then catalog proxy hosted on Cloudflare Workers. The proxy requests public catalog information from TheTVDB and may cache public responses for performance.

The proxy is not designed to receive your notes, ratings, tags, people, collections or other private journal content. Cloudflare, TheTVDB and internet-service providers may process standard network information required to route, secure and troubleshoot requests, such as an IP address, request time, device or user-agent information and error details.

Poster, backdrop and cast images may load from TheTVDB artwork addresses and may be cached by your device or the catalog infrastructure.

Notifications and system features

And Then asks for notification permission only when you choose a feature that needs it, such as a watch reminder. Reminders are scheduled through iOS. The app may also use Apple system features such as Spotlight indexing or share sheets when you enable or invoke them.

Optional purchases

Optional tips and the Founding Supporter purchase are processed by Apple through StoreKit. And Then receives product and transaction status needed to complete or restore the purchase. It does not receive your complete payment-card information.

Consumable tips do not unlock the Founding Supporter mark. The non-consumable Founding Supporter purchase adds a permanent, restorable in-app badge.

Exports, backups and deletion

You can create backups or exports and share them to a destination you choose. Once exported, those files are controlled by you and by the destination you select.

You can delete individual records or erase the local archive from inside the app. Removing the app may also remove local data that has not been exported or backed up.

This website

This static website does not set advertising cookies and does not include analytics scripts. The hosting provider may process standard request information needed to deliver and protect the pages.

If you email support, the message, email address and any information you choose to include will be used to respond to the request.

Your choices

  • Use the app without creating an account
  • Decline notifications or change permission in iOS Settings
  • Choose whether to export or share an archive
  • Delete records or erase the local archive
  • Avoid live catalog requests by using already-saved local records

Children

And Then is a general-audience entertainment journal and is not designed to create accounts or profiles for children. The app does not knowingly request a child’s name, address or contact details.

Changes to this policy

This page may be updated when the app, service providers or business model changes. The effective date at the top will be revised when material updates are published.
