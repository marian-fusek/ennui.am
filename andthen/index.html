A few direct answers

Before the beginning.

Is And Then a social network?

No. The product is a private journal first. Your notes, ratings and memories are not published as a profile or feed.

Does it work for series too?

Yes. You can find movies and series, keep seasons or episodes in context and record the experience that mattered.

What happens when I do not remember the date?

Choose that you do not remember. The experience remains in your archive and appears after dated entries rather than being assigned a false day.

Will it require a subscription?

No subscription is planned for the core archive. Optional one-time tips and a Founding Supporter purchase may be available.
